My Story

I'm Sunil Kumawat, an enthusiastic Mathematics Educator at Jayshree Periwal International School in Jaipur. With a rich academic background and diverse experience, Ph.D. in Mathematics from Manipal University. As an Academic Principal at Scholars International School, I focused on curriculum development and effective teaching methods. I also served as the Head of Department at Air Force School, emphasizing student performance analysis and improvement.

In addition to my teaching roles, I am a Microsoft Certified Educator, a member of "The Association of Mathematics Teachers of India," and a subject expert at SGC Education & Welfare Society. I have authored papers published in national and international journals and hold patents in innovation.

My commitment to professional development is evident through my participation in various workshops, certifications, and computer proficiency. I strive to connect with students through a scientific approach, aiming to be an accepted educator for all. My dedication to education and continuous learning reflects in my achievements and contributions to the academic community.
